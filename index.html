Akan Names

Depending on when you were born, the Akan give you a name hence the name "Akan Names" These names give meaning to one's life.

Here are the male names depending on when you were born

Male Names

  1. Sunday - Kwasi
  2. Monday - Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday - Kwabena
  4. Wednesday - Kwaku
  5. Thursday - Yaw
  6. Friday - Kofi
  7. Saturday - Kwame

Female Names.

  1. Sunday - Akosua
  2. Monday - Adwoa
  3. Tuesday -Abenaa
  4. Wednesday - Akua
  5. Thursday - Yaa
  6. Friday - Afua
  7. Saturday - Ama
